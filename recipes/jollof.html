Jollof Rice Recipe

Home

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. It is often served with fried plantains, salad, and your choice of protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes or 4 large fresh tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 large onion (divided)
  • 2-3 red bell peppers
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnet peppers (optional, for heat)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 2-3 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, bell peppers, half the onion, garlic, and Scotch bonnet into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pot. Dice the remaining onion and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add tomato paste and fry for 2-3 minutes. Add the blended mixture and cook until reduced and oil separates (about 10-15 minutes).
  4. Stir in thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add washed rice and stir to coat with the sauce.
  6. Pour in stock to cover the rice. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed (about 20-30 minutes). Add more stock if needed.
  7. Fluff rice and serve with fried plantains, salad, and your choice of protein.
